About BioMind

An open educational project that pairs clear science with modern AI to make the human body and mind accessible to everyone.

🎯 Our Mission

BioMind exists to lower the barrier to high-quality knowledge about biology, neuroscience, psychology, and wellbeing. Too often, these subjects feel locked behind jargon or expensive textbooks. We want to change that.

By combining concise topic summaries with an on-page AI assistant, BioMind lets you learn at your own pace, ask follow-up questions in plain language, and connect ideas across different fields of science.

💻 The Technology

BioMind is a static web application hosted on Vercel and version-controlled on GitHub.

The AI assistant is powered by Mistral 7B Instruct via OpenRouter. User queries are routed through a secure Vercel serverless function — the API key is never exposed in the browser.

Vercel GitHub OpenRouter Mistral 7B Vanilla JS HTML/CSS
