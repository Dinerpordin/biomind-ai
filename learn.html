Learning Pathways

Structured tracks that take you from the basics of cells and neurons all the way to consciousness, behaviour, and wellbeing.

📚 Structured Learning Tracks

1
Foundations of Life & the Body
⏱ 3–4 hours  •  Level: Beginner
  • What is a cell? Organelles, tissues, and organs
  • Major organ systems and how they connect
  • Homeostasis — the body’s balancing act
  • DNA basics: genes, chromosomes, and heredity
Start with Cell Biology, Human Anatomy, and Genetics & Evolution on the Explore page.
2
Brain, Neurons & Nervous System
⏱ 4–6 hours  •  Level: Intermediate
  • Neuron anatomy and how action potentials fire
  • Synaptic transmission and neurotransmitters
  • Brain regions: cortex, limbic system, cerebellum
  • Neuroplasticity and how the brain rewires itself
  • Sleep stages, circadian rhythms, and dreaming
Combine Brain Structure, Neurons & Synapses, and Sleep & Dreams.
3
Mind, Behaviour & Psychology
⏱ 4–5 hours  •  Level: Intermediate
  • How emotions arise and influence decisions
  • Cognitive biases and how perception shapes reality
  • Social dynamics, influence, and group behaviour
  • Memory encoding, storage, and retrieval
Explore Emotions & Cognition, Social Psychology, and Learning & Memory.
4
Wellness & the Brain-Body Connection
⏱ 3–4 hours  •  Level: Foundational–Intermediate
  • The gut-brain axis and microbiome research
  • Exercise neuroscience: BDNF and mood
  • Mindfulness and its measurable brain effects
  • Nutrition, inflammation, and cognitive performance
Use Mindfulness & Meditation, Exercise & Movement, and Nutrition & Brain Health.
